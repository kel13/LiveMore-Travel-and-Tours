About Us

Livemore Travel and Tours is a travel organizer, for joiners and groups alike.

We cater to all, families, friends, company outings and weary travellers willing to jump right in to a sea of strangers to make new friends.

Founded in 2009, the company is founded by Ren-ren Tacasa in Sampaloc, Manila. We are now featured in iWitness for Joiner's Tour.
